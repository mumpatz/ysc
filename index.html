Our Vision

A Sacred Pause:
Re-Wild Your Spirit

A Yoga Spirit Circle retreat is more than a getaway; it is a sacred pause—a 're-wilding' of the self. It is a gathering of souls dedicated to profound nourishment, creativity, and connection.

We believe in the alchemy of nature, art, and spiritual wisdom. Our mission is to uplift individuals by bringing them together to create conscious communities. Since our founding in 2013, our circle has expanded rapidly, weaving a tapestry of lasting friendships between guests, teachers, and our talented team of staff and volunteers.

The vibe created here is relaxed, peaceful, kind, and fun. It is a space where you can simply be.

Based in Berlin, Yvonne is an entrepreneurial spirit dedicated to living life to the fullest. Whether exploring new destinations or enjoying techno music, she believes laughter and adventure are essential for growth. A self-confessed foodie, Yvonne curates our culinary experiences, hiring the best plant-based chefs to ensure our menus are as nourishing as they are delicious.

Living on the Costa del Sol near our Andalusian venues, Isabel blends a rational engineering background with a deep love for spirituality. Passionate about interior design and pottery, she creates spaces that are both beautiful and functional. A natural host, Isabel finds joy in creating warm, welcoming environments. She stays grounded through yoga, swimming, and dancing.

Our vision truly comes alive through the amazing humans we meet. It is the unique mix of inspiring teachers, talented chefs, and open-hearted guests who fill this circle with energy. Together, they create a vibe that is relaxed, kind, and deeply connected. We cherish the lasting friendships formed here and look forward to welcoming you into this vibrant community.
